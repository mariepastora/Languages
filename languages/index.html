In 2016, this is what language diversity looked like in Astoria.

English and Spanish were predominant, as this can easily be seen. Many languages such as French, Tagalog or Chinese were each spoken by about 1% of the people living in Astoria.

Greek was the third most spoken language at home, but this only accounted for 4% of the total population of the neighborhood.
